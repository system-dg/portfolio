Why is my device not complaint when I've done everything I'm supposed to do?

The compliance status in My device is not updated instantaneously. Changes that you make to your device can take up to one day to be reflected in My devices.

What happens if a device is not complaint with the security requirements?

If you have a non-complaint device, you are notified via email that you must take action. The mail specifies the steps you need to take to make the device compliant and you have a 30-day grace period
