KB0011014

Important Changes to Texas University Login Credentials and Password Reset

John . 10 view . 10mo ago .

1) Texas students, faculty and staff will no longer reset their passwords through Campus connect.

2) Texas faculty, staff and student employees must register for BlueKey multi-factor authentication in order to access the new BlueSky portal (Oracle Cloud HR and Finance system). Once they have registered, they will be able to reset their passwords without contacting the Help Desk

3) It is highly recommended that DePaul students register for self-service password reset. This is very similar to the process to register for BlueKey multi-factor authentication. Once they have registered, they will be able to reset their passwords without contacting the Help Desk.

Helpful links:

Register now for BlueKey multi-factor authentication (or self-service password reset if you are a student).

https://bluekey.depaul.edu/multifactorsetup

Step-by-step guide to registering for multi-factor authentication (self-service password reset registration is very similar).

https://depaul.service-now.com/sp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0011009

Reset your password once you have registered:

https://bluekey.depaul.edu/pwresetor https://go.depaul.edu/passwordreset.

Step-by-step guide to Bluekey self-service password reset:

https://depaul.service-now.com/sp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0011005
